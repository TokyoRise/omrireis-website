Tencent Games Japan — New Brand Strategy

The Project

A new Japanese gaming brand built from the ground up.

This project was a proposal for Tencent Games to develop a new Japanese gaming brand with a unique strategy and identity for the Japanese market. The work combined deep cultural research with brand positioning and visual identity development tailored specifically for Japanese gamers.
