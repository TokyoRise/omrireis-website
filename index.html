About

Based in Tel Aviv after nearly two decades working globally.

I've spent my career across Tokyo, New York, and Israel — working for some of the world's most culturally significant brands at agencies including AKQA, Huge, Flamingo, and the DIFI group. I speak 3 languages and believe stories are the most powerful force in the universe.
